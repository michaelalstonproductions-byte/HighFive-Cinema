# HighFive Cinema Press Kit

## Positioning

HighFive Cinema is a streaming and creator platform for premium cinematic discovery, creator publishing, and audience engagement.

## One-Line Description

HighFive Cinema gives viewers a premium streaming experience while giving creators tools to publish, manage, analyze, and grow their work.

## Product Pillars

- Premium streaming experience
- Creator publishing pipeline
- Discovery and recommendations
- Creator profiles and collaboration
- Series and episodes
- Viewer library and progress
- Analytics and revenue insight
- Rights, moderation, and launch operations

## Launch Narrative

HighFive Cinema brings the viewer experience and creator workflow into one product: creators can prepare and publish work, viewers can discover and watch it, and the platform can measure, operate, and govern the content lifecycle.

## Screenshot Captions

- Home: cinematic discovery and featured titles
- Search: discovery, creators, collections, and title search
- Library: saved titles, progress, favorites, and history
- Creator: publishing workspace, assets, analytics, and revenue
- Operations: review, rights, moderation, beta, and launch operations
- Player: premium playback shell and content context
