# HighFive Cinema Support Runbook

## Support Intake

Initial support categories:

- Account access
- Playback issue
- Creator publishing issue
- Upload or processing issue
- Subscription or entitlement issue
- Library or download issue
- Rights availability issue
- Safety or moderation report

## Response Priority

- P0: widespread app launch failure, account lockout, or playback outage
- P1: creator publishing blocked, entitlement mismatch, or corrupted library state
- P2: single-user playback issue, search issue, profile issue, or creator workspace issue
- P3: copy, visual polish, metadata, or documentation issue

## First Response Targets

- P0: same business day
- P1: one business day
- P2: two business days
- P3: five business days

## Required Triage Fields

- App version
- Build number
- Device model
- OS version
- Account role
- Route or screen
- Content title or project ID
- Reproduction steps
- Screenshot or screen recording when available

## Escalation Owners

- Viewer playback and library: Product engineering
- Creator publishing and uploads: Creator platform engineering
- Account and entitlement: Platform engineering
- Moderation and rights: Operations
- App Store listing or public launch: Release owner

## Launch Support Checklist

- Confirm support inbox ownership.
- Confirm support macros for viewer, creator, and admin issues.
- Confirm privacy/data export escalation.
- Confirm account deletion escalation.
- Confirm creator publishing rollback path.
- Confirm incident channel and on-call owner.
