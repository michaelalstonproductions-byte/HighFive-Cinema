# HighFive Cinema Terms of Use Draft

This draft is a launch operations artifact and requires legal review before public release.

## Service

HighFive Cinema provides a streaming and creator platform for discovering, viewing, publishing, and managing cinematic content.

## Accounts

Users are responsible for maintaining control of their account and for activity that occurs through their account.

## Creator Content

Creators are responsible for ensuring that submitted content, artwork, trailers, metadata, and related materials are accurate and that they have the required rights to distribute them.

## Content Availability

Content availability may vary by rights window, territory, entitlement, moderation decision, or platform policy.

## Viewer Library

Saved titles, viewing progress, downloads, favorites, and history may depend on account state, entitlement state, and content availability.

## Payments and Entitlements

Purchases, restores, billing status, and entitlements must be handled through supported Apple platform purchase flows and backend entitlement records.

## Moderation

HighFive may review, restrict, remove, or request revision of content that violates platform policy, rights requirements, or safety requirements.

## Data Rights

Users may request account deletion and data export through supported account workflows.

## Availability

The service may change, pause, or remove features during beta, launch, maintenance, or incident response.
